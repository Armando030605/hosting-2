Internet use in Mexico

In Mexico there are 80.6 million Internet users, representing 70.1% of the population aged six years or more, this figure reveals an increase of 4.3 percentage points compared to that registered in 2018 (65.8%) and of 12.7 percentage points compared to 2015 (57.4 percent) the number of households with Internet access is estimated at 20.1 million(56.4%).


Age and sex of the Users

  1. 51.6% are women
  2. 48.4% are men
Between 2017 and 2019, users in the urban area went from 71.2% to 76.6%, while in the rural area the increase was from 39.2% to 47.7%.

Internet access points

Some were connected through a fixed or mobile connection, which means an increase of 3.5 percentage points compared to 2018 and 17.2 percentage points compared to the results of 2015 (39.2 percent), the three main means for the connection of Internet users in 2019 were: smart cell phone (Smartphone) with 95.3%; laptop with 33.2%, and desktop computer with 28.9 percent.

Internet user activities

The main activities of Internet users in 2019 corresponded to entertainment (91.5%), obtaining information (90.7%) and communication (90.6 percent)

As extra data, Internet users identified as main problems when connecting to the network, the slowness in the transfer of information (50.1%), interruptions in the service (38.6%) and excess.
